ABOUT ME

My Name is Brian Aginga Madiang. I was born in the year 1999 in a family of nine children five girls and 4 boys. I am the seventh born and Last born in the boys category. My education journey started in Burumba primary school in Busia, where I attented both lower school and Middle school. I then proceeded to high school later on and joined st Mathias Secondary school Busia.

After finishing High school, I got a chance to Join Amboseli school of Hospitality. But due to lack of enough funds to support my higher education the opportunity did not came to pass. I spent gap year trying to figure out what I can do with my life. With the help of God I found this lady who introduces me to matter technology, and I believe that was the path that was design for me.

I applied for schorlaships from google platforms, after two weeks my schorlashipwas approved and I started my higher Education on IT SUPPORT courses for 1 year. After that I went into Programming where I did Python Programming and Data Science courses offered by IBM for one and have years. And later on I enroll to become Fronted Engineer.

I had envisioned my self as a person who will one day Travel the world and be part and parcel of the Aviation Family. I have made several trips a cross the wprld trying to advocate for (SAATAM) single African Air Market. I have always believe that for African connectivity to grow we must come up with policies that allows African member states citizens to move freely without a VISA being a prerequisite in some
African Borders.
